Introduction

With more than 20 years in the creative field, I am an experienced App Product Designer and a skilled Individual Contributor. My expertise spans Strategy, Research, User Experience and Visual Design.

At the heart of my work is my passion for designing meaningful products that address real customer needs while connecting business objectives with customer satisfaction. My expertise is founded upon a user-centric design approach further enriched by my diverse experiences across various sectors, including finance, transport, health, government, and telecommunications.
