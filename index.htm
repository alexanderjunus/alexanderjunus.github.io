Introduction

I am a Product Designer with thirteen years’ experience in many facets of creative work that covers a spectrum from UX to Visual Design. Having obtained my Bachelor's degree in Design and a Master's degree in Human-Computer Interaction, my design approach is a user-centric methodology.

My capabilities are coupled with extensive working experience in a wide variety of industries including finance, health, government bodies and telecommunications.

My innate ability to empathise with the user enable me to internalise their feeling which motivates me to alleviate their problems and to design products that are usable, lasting and elevate human experience.
